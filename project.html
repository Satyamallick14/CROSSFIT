Strength Building

💪 Lean Muscle Building

Goal: Build muscle while minimizing fat gain

Calorie Intake: 5-10% surplus above maintenance

Macronutrients:

  • Protein: 1.2-1.6g per kg body weight
  • Carbs: 3-5g per kg (whole grains, fruits)
  • Fats: 0.8-1g per kg (healthy sources)

Sample Meal Plan:

  • 🍳 Breakfast: Greek yogurt with berries and chia seeds
  • 🥪 Lunch: Turkey breast sandwich with avocado
  • 🥗 Snack: Cottage cheese with vegetables
  • 🍤 Dinner: Shrimp stir-fry with brown rice

Fat Loss

🔥 Fat Loss Program

Goal: Reduce body fat while maintaining muscle

Calorie Intake: 10-20% deficit below maintenance

Macronutrients:

  • Protein: 1.2-1.6g per kg (lean sources)
  • Carbs: 2-4g per kg (vegetables, fruits)
  • Fats: 0.8-1g per kg (healthy fats)

Sample Meal Plan:

  • 🍳 Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach
  • 🥗 Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with quinoa
  • 🥕 Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus
  • 🐟 Dinner: Baked cod with roasted vegetables

Weight Gain

📈 Bulking Program

Goal: Increase muscle mass and overall weight

Calorie Intake: 15-20% surplus above maintenance

Macronutrients:

  • Protein: 1.6-2.2g per kg body weight
  • Carbs: 4-6g per kg (whole grains, starchy vegetables)
  • Fats: 0.8-1g per kg (nuts, seeds, oils)

Sample Meal Plan:

  • 🥣 Breakfast: Oatmeal with protein powder and nuts
  • 🍗 Lunch: Chicken breast with brown rice
  • 🥤 Snack: Protein shake with banana
  • 🐟 Dinner: Salmon with quinoa and broccoli
